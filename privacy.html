Privacy Policy

DrivePrep NSW · Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

DrivePrep NSW ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using SwiftData:

  • Your practice session history and answers
  • Mock test results and scores
  • Bookmarked questions
  • Progress statistics and accuracy metrics

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Advertising (Free Tier)

The free version of the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage information to serve personalised ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

In-App Purchases

If you make a purchase through the App, your payment information is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data Security

Since all your practice data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. The App does not transmit your practice history, test scores, or any personal information to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for learner drivers preparing for the NSW Driver Knowledge Test. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App's local storage does not require or collect personal identifying information.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can clear all your practice history and statistics through the Stats screen
  • Uninstall: Deleting the App will permanently remove all locally stored data from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Not Affiliated

This App is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Service NSW, Transport for NSW, or any government agency. This is an independent study tool.
