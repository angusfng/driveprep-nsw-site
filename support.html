Before You Email

Try these first — they fix most issues:

  • Close and reopen the app – resolves most display glitches
  • Update the app – go to the App Store and check for updates
  • Restore purchases – if your premium access disappeared, go to the Paywall screen and tap "Restore Purchases"

When Emailing Us, Please Include

  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Your iPhone model
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Any screenshots if relevant
